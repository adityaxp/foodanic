Coconut and Mawa Ladoo Recipe:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup of condensed milk
- 100 g of mawa/khoya
- 1 cup of desiccated coconut
- 1/2 cup of chopped almonds
- 1/2 cup of chopped cashews

Instructions:

1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, add condensed milk and mawa/khoya and mix well.

2. Heat the mixture on medium flame until it starts to thicken. Stir occasionally to prevent burning.

3. Reduce the flame to low and add the desiccated coconut to the pan.

4. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes or until the mixture thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.

5. Add chopped almonds and cashews to the pan and mix well.

6. Turn off the flame and let the mixture cool to room temperature.

7. Once the mixture is cool, take a small amount and roll it into a ball shape with your palms.

8. Roll the ball in desiccated coconut to coat it completely.

9. Repeat the process with the remaining mixture.

10. Your delicious coconut and mawa ladoos are ready to serve!

Enjoy your coconut and mawa ladoos as a dessert or snack. You can also store them in an airtight container for up to a week.
