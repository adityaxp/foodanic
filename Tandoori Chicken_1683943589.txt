Step-by-step Recipe Guide for Greek Yogurt Marinade with Garam Masala, Kasuri Methi, and Mustard Oil:

Ingredients:
- 1 cup Greek Yogurt
- 1 tablespoon garam masala
- 1 tablespoon kasuri methi
- 2 tablespoons mustard oil
- 1 tablespoon marinade (optional)

Step 1: In a medium-sized bowl, whisk the Greek yogurt until it is smooth.

Step 2: Add the garam masala and kasuri methi to the bowl, and mix the spices into the yogurt thoroughly.

Step 3: Pour the mustard oil into the bowl with the yogurt mixture, and whisk everything together until there are no lumps and the marinade is smooth.

Step 4: Optional: If you want to use a pre-made marinade to enhance the flavor of your dish, mix in one tablespoon of your preferred marinade into the yogurt mixture.

Step 5: You can use this marinade to marinate meats, fish, or vegetables for at least 30 minutes before cooking. Brush the marinade on your ingredient, cover, and let it chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes to infuse the flavors.

Step 6: When you are ready to cook your ingredient, discard any leftover marinade and cook the food as desired.

Enjoy your delicious, flavorful meal with this tasty Greek yogurt marinade!
